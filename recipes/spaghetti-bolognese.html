Veggie Loaded Spaghetti Bolognese



Veggie loaded Spaghetti Bolognese full of nutrition from both beef and veggie content

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Onion
  • Tinned tomatoes
  • Tomatoe paste
  • Carrot
  • Zucchini
  • Beef mince
  • Spinach
  • Garlic & onion powder herbs
  • Mushrooms

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Grate the carrot, zucchini and mushrooms
  2. Finely dice the onion, you can choose to grate the onion as well
  3. In a large pan heat 2 tablespoon of olive oil. Sauté the onion, grated carrot, and zucchini until softened and starting to go golden
  4. Add the tomato paste, sauté for 2-3 minutes this helps deepen the flavour
  5. Add the mince and the mushrooms, cook until the beef mince is browned
  6. Add the tinned tomatoes, dried herbs and spinach.
  7. Simmer for 25-30 mins until everything is well cooked, the sauce has thickened and flavourful While the spag bol is simmering cook your chosen pasta as per the packet directions
  8. Serve with cooked pasta
