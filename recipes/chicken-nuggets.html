Chicken Nuggets with Sweet Potato


Healthy and delicious Chicken Nuggets even the picky eaters will love

Ingredients

  • 500g Lean chicken mince or ground chicken breast
  • 2 Sweet Potatos(Butternut squash may be used in place if preferred)
  • 1 cup Bread Crumbs
  • Salt (Optional)
  • Onion Powder (Optional flavour additon)

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Peel, cut and cook sweet potatos ahead of time.
  2. Mash the cooled potato
  3. Add the potato to a bowl with the minced chicken and salt. Add onion powder if you’d like
  4. Form into small balls, flatten slightly, and coat in breadcrumbs.
  5. Place onto a parchment-lined baking sheet and bake for 45-60 minutes at 200ºc
